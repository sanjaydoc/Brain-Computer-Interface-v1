The four parts

One loop, closed in simulation first.

Molecules make neurons ultrasound-sensitive; the scanner writes with focused ultrasound and reads with neural dust; the template says which neurons carry the machinery; the virtual environment runs the whole loop and shows it live.

1

Molecular engineering

De-novo biomolecule design (De-Novo-LLM) generates the sonogenetic ion channels that make target neurons respond to ultrasound.

2

Electronics & hardware

An ultrasound scanner: sonogenetics to write (open the channels) and neural dust to read (ultrasonic backscatter of neural activity).

3

Brain template

A living digital twin of a connectome — pluggable source (worm / synthetic / MICrONS mouse), multi-scale toward the human brain.

4

Virtual environment

Simulate the twin, inject stimulus, read activity back, and watch neurons fire in real time in the 3D view.

The scale ladder

302 neurons to 86 billion — the same code.

Two principles bind every module: configurability (config selects implementations) and a scalability contract (Structure-of-Arrays + sparse connectivity + partition-native, nothing worse than O(E)). Scaling up is loading the next profile, not a rewrite.
